# MedPup Brand Style Guide

> **Version:** 1.0 | **Last Updated:** May 2026  
> **Purpose:** Consistent visual identity across all MedPup touchpoints

---

## 1. Brand Foundation

### Brand Name
**MedPup** (capital M, capital P — one word, no space)

### Tagline
**World-class veterinary care. A short trip away.**

### Brand Voice
Compassionate, knowledgeable, transparent, reassuring. Never casual about medical risk.

**Do:**
- Use warm, empathetic language
- Lead with data and facts
- Acknowledge the seriousness of surgery
- Emphasize trust and safety

**Don't:**
- Use slang or casual tone about medical procedures
- Make promises about outcomes
- Sound like a discount service (we're a value service with premium standards)

## 3. Typography

### Primary Font: Inter
Available for free at [fonts.google.com](https://fonts.google.com/specimen/Inter)

| Weight | Usage |
|--------|-------|
| Inter Bold (700) | Headings, hero text, price figures |
| Inter Semi-Bold (600) | Subheadings, navigation, buttons |
| Inter Regular (400) | Body text |
| Inter Light (300) | Secondary info, captions |

### Legal Font: Times New Roman
For legal documents, contracts, and disclosures only.

### Sizing Scale

| Element | Size | Weight |
|---------|------|--------|
| Hero heading | 2.5rem / 40px | Bold (700) |
| Page title | 2rem / 32px | Bold (700) |
| Section heading | 1.5rem / 24px | Semi-Bold (600) |
| Subheading | 1.25rem / 20px | Semi-Bold (600) |
| Body text | 1rem / 16px | Regular (400) |
| Small / caption | 0.875rem / 14px | Regular (400) |
| Legal disclaimer | 0.75rem / 12px | Regular (400) |

## 4. Logo

### Primary Logo
- **File:** `assets/brand/logo.svg`
- **Usage:** Website header, print materials, social media profiles
- **Clear space:** Minimum 20px on all sides (or height of the "M" in MEDPUP)

### Favicon
- **File:** `assets/brand/favicon.svg`
- **Usage:** Browser tab icon, bookmarks
- **Description:** Simplified paw with compass east indicator on deep blue circle

### Logo Misuse
- Do not stretch or distort
- Do not change colors
- Do not add effects (shadows, gradients beyond original)
- Do not place on busy backgrounds without sufficient contrast
- Do not rearrange elements

## 5. Imagery Style

### Photography
- Warm, natural lighting
- Dogs as heroes — show their personality and dignity
- Include human-animal bond moments
- Clean, uncluttered backgrounds
- Avoid clinical/sterile looks — show care, not just equipment

### Iconography
- Line icons, consistent stroke width (2px)
- Teal (#319795) on light backgrounds
- White on dark backgrounds
- Preferred icon set: Feather Icons (open source)

---

## 6. Tone Guidelines

### Website Copy
- Headlines: Problem-aware, benefit-driven
  - *"Your dog deserves the best care. It shouldn't require a second mortgage."*
- Body: Empathetic, data-supported, clear
- CTAs: Direct and warm
  - *"Book Your Free Consultation"* not *"Submit"*

### Email
- Subject lines: Personal, specific
  - *"Your trip to Freeport is confirmed!"*
  - *"How is Bella recovering?"*
- Body: Conversational but professional

### Social Media
- Compassionate, community-oriented
- Share real stories and savings numbers
- Educational content > promotional content

## 7. Legal Disclaimers

Standard disclaimer for all client-facing materials:

> *MedPup is a veterinary logistics concierge, not a veterinary practice. All medical services are provided by independently licensed veterinarians.*

Must appear on:
- Website footer
- All service pages
- All pricing pages
- All email signatures
- All marketing materials
