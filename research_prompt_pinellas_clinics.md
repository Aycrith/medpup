## FULL CONTEXT

### The Business

A Pinellas County, Florida-based software engineer wants to build a business routing cost-constrained pet owners to lower-cost veterinary care. Largo, FL is the home base and launch market.

### Research Completed So Far (Don't Re-Search These)

All of the following are already verified and do not need to be researched again:

**Texas Land Border (verified 76.8% savings):** Vet Playas Tijuana publishes prices: spay $90-120, dental $250-350, cruciate repair $950-1,200 vs US $4,500-6,500. MexiVet Express does 80 pets/week since 2018. Screwworm certificate (Nov 2024) adds ~$30-80 friction. This is the highest-margin option but requires Texas presence or concierge transport add-on.

**Puerto Rico (verified ~13.7% savings via Banfield corporate pricing):** Structural factors (Jones Act, 2x mainland utility rates, vet shortage of 200-300 vets for 3M people, 11.5% IVU sales tax) prevent deeper savings. Not a viable stand-alone model but useful as an add-on.

**Georgia (verified ~10% statewide, ~37.6% Miami→rural GA extreme case):** The South Georgia Low Cost Spay & Neuter Clinic (Thomasville) offers spay/neuter at 65-85% below FL rates. This is a single-clinic, single-procedure nonprofit — not a generalizable business model.

**Dr. Kelly's Surgical Unit (validated end-game model):** 7 AZ locations + Denver expansion. Dog spay $160-$362, cat spay $102-$125, dental ~$900. Prices = 1/3 to 1/2 of full-service. No equivalent exists in Florida — open market.

**Legal structure:** Florida LLC for operations/referral business. Flat admin/coordination fees are safe. Per-procedure commissions are not. MSO structure (you own ops, vet owns medicine) for Phase 2 clinic.

**Consumer willingness:** 71% of cost-constrained owners never offered cheaper alternative (Gallup 2025). Owners won't travel themselves (2-6 mile radius) but WILL hand pets to concierge if savings are 50%+.

**South Florida (Miami-Dade/Broward/Palm Beach) low-cost clinic landscape — DO NOT RE-RESEARCH:** 22 clinics identified. 5 high-likelihood partner clinics (Good Care Animal Clinic, Heroes Veterinary Hospital, Paws 2 Help, Justin Bartlett, Paws2Health). ASPCA CVC dog spay $80 (80-90% savings). Good Care dental $250-$350 (83-94% savings). This data applies to South Florida only, NOT to Pinellas County.

### What's Different About Largo/Pinellas County

The operator is in Largo, FL (Pinellas County, Tampa Bay area). This is a completely different market from South Florida:
- Different low-cost vet clinics
- Different demographics (older population, different income distribution)
- Closer to Georgia (Tampa to Thomasville GA = ~4.5 hours vs Miami to Thomasville = ~7 hours)
- Has Tampa International Airport (TPA) — flights to Puerto Rico, Texas, etc.
- Different commercial real estate market
- Part of the I-75 corridor to Georgia

## RESEARCH PROMPT: Pinellas County / Tampa Bay Low-Cost Veterinary Landscape

### Goal

Find every low-cost veterinary clinic, spay/neuter clinic, dental service, surgical center, or mobile vet operating in Pinellas County (Largo, Clearwater, St. Petersburg) and surrounding areas (Hillsborough County/Tampa, Pasco County) that offers prices below full-service market rates. Also find pricing data, capacity signals, and which clinics might accept referral partnerships.
