## Operating Principle

The fastest legal path is to become the best coordination layer around existing licensed veterinary and rescue infrastructure, not to recreate that infrastructure immediately.

## First 30-Day Outcome Target

By the end of 30 days, the project should have:

- one defined pilot zone,
- a public landing page,
- a working intake form,
- a case board,
- at least 20 verified resource entries,
- at least 20 outreach attempts,
- emergency red-flag routing,
- no public fundraising unless compliance is cleared,
- no animal housing,
- no diagnosis/treatment/prescription activity,
- 3–10 documented pilot cases,
- and a written pilot review.
