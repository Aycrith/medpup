# Dog Imports: Check Country Disease Status

Last Modified: January 21, 2026

The U.S. Centers for Disease Control and Prevention updated rabies vaccination requirements for all dogs entering or returning to the United States. Certain requirements must be met _**before**_**departing the United States**.[Learn More](http://www.aphis.usda.gov/pet-travel/another-country-to-us-import/dogs/disease-status#modal-link-158)

APHIS Veterinary Services, along with the CDC, has specific requirements for dogs traveling (imported) to the United States from countries affected by particular diseases such as foot-and-mouth disease (FMD),screwworm, and rabies.

**For dog(s) coming from...**

*   FMD-affected countries (not free), follow the [APHIS Veterinary Services import requirements.](http://www.aphis.usda.gov/pet-travel/another-country-to-us-import/dogs "Bring a Pet Dog into the United States")
