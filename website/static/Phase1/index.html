Frequently Asked Questions

Is this safe? Are the clinics legitimate?

Yes. Every clinic we route to is:
• Operated by a licensed Florida veterinarian
• Accredited and inspected
• Independently owned — we do not own or employ any clinic
• Offering prices that are publicly listed on their own websites

How much does MedPup charge?

A flat coordination fee per appointment: $15–$50 for wellness/spay, $50–$100 for dental and surgical procedures. No ongoing subscriptions. No percentage markup on the procedure bill.

Why are these clinics cheaper?

Low-cost clinics operate on a high-volume, thin-margin non-profit or mission-driven model. They accept government funding and grants, which subsidizes care for low-income residents. The care quality is identical — the same licensed vets, the same anesthetics, the same surgical standards — at a fraction of the cost.

Do I need to travel internationally?

Not for Phase 1. All our current partner clinics are within 1 hour of Largo, FL. No ferry, no customs, no international paperwork.

Can I use CareCredit?

If the partner clinic accepts CareCredit, yes — you pay the clinic directly. MedPup is not involved in payment processing.

How do you make money?

We charge a transparent flat coordination fee for our time: finding prices, confirming appointments, sending reminders, and coordinating records between you and the clinic. The clinic bills you directly for the procedure — we never markup medical fees.
