Frequently Asked Questions

What does the MedPup fee actually cover?

The flat coordination fee covers: realistic cost calculation based on your pet's specific needs, clinic price verification through direct calls, appointment reservation, and our surprise-bill guarantee. The clinic's bill goes directly to the clinic — MedPup never touches the procedure cost.

What if the final bill exceeds your quote?

We take responsibility. If the final clinic bill exceeds our confirmed all-in range, we cover the difference (up to the cost of your coordination fee) and find you a backup clinic at no extra charge. No disputes, no fine print — it's in your service agreement.

Do I have to travel far?

Current partner clinics are 20–55 minutes from Largo by car. No customs, no APHIS paperwork, no ferry or flight required. We route you to the closest clinic that offers your needed procedure at the best confirmed price.

What about follow-up care?

Your regular veterinarian handles all post-op rechecks. We provide them a complete surgical record from the partner clinic and remain available by phone throughout your pet's recovery.
