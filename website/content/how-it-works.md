Getting your pet the surgery they need shouldn't mean emptying your savings. MedPup makes it simple — here's exactly how it works.

<div class="step">
<div class="step-number">01</div>
<div class="step-content">
<h3>Get Your U.S. Vet's Diagnosis</h3>
<p>Start with your regular veterinarian in Florida. You'll need a diagnosis, recommended procedure, and any relevant medical records. We work with your vet — not around them.</p>
</div>
</div>

<div class="step">
<div class="step-number">02</div>
<div class="step-content">
<h3>Free Consultation &amp; Quote</h3>
<p>Schedule a free 15-minute call with a MedPup concierge. We'll review your pet's diagnosis, discuss the best destination (Freeport day-trip or Cancun surgical trip), and provide a transparent all-in cost estimate — including the clinic's surgery fee and our concierge fee.</p>
</div>
</div>

<div class="step">
<div class="step-number">03</div>
<div class="step-content">
<h3>Choose Your Package</h3>
<p>Select your trip type: a same-day ferry trip to Freeport, Bahamas for dental, mass removal, or soft tissue surgery, or a 2-4 night trip to Cancun, Mexico for complex orthopedics (TPLO, IVDD), oncology, or advanced diagnostics.</p>
</div>
</div>

<div class="step">
<div class="step-number">04</div>
<div class="step-content">
<h3>We Handle Everything</h3>
<p>Once booked, MedPup manages 100% of the logistics: USDA health certificates, Bahamas import permits (Click2Clear), ferry or flight bookings, partner clinic scheduling, and ground transport. You just show up.</p>
</div>
</div>

<div class="step">
<div class="step-number">05</div>
<div class="step-content">
<h3>Trip Day — Concierge by Your Side</h3>
<p>Your MedPup concierge meets you at the terminal and stays with you and your pet the entire day — from check-in through surgery to return. You're never alone, and we handle any surprises.</p>
</div>
</div>

<div class="step">
<div class="step-number">06</div>
<div class="step-content">
<h3>Post-Op Care &amp; Follow-Up</h3>
<p>After surgery, you receive complete post-op instructions, medications, and our 24-hour hotline. Your primary U.S. vet handles the 14-day follow-up recheck (we provide them the full surgical report). Your pet comes home healthier, and you keep thousands in your pocket.</p>
</div>
</div>
