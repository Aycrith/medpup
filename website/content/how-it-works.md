Getting your pet the care they need shouldn't mean emptying your savings. MedPup makes it simple — here's exactly how it works.

<div class="step">
<div class="step-number">01</div>
<div class="step-content">
<h3>Get Your Vet's Diagnosis</h3>
<p>Start with your regular veterinarian in the Tampa Bay area. You'll need a diagnosis, recommended procedure, and any relevant medical records. We work with your vet — not around them.</p>
</div>
</div>

<div class="step">
<div class="step-number">02</div>
<div class="step-content">
<h3>Free Consultation & Quote</h3>
<p>Schedule a free 15-minute call with a MedPup coordinator. We'll review your pet's diagnosis, identify the lowest-cost clinic within driving distance, and provide a transparent all-in cost estimate — including the clinic's fee and our coordination fee.</p>
</div>
</div>

<div class="step">
<div class="step-number">03</div>
<div class="step-content">
<h3>We Route You to Verified Pricing</h3>
<p>We present 1–3 clinic options with published or confirmed pricing. Every price is sourced by our pricing-intel tool before you see it. No guesswork, no bill-shock.</p>
</div>
</div>

<div class="step">
<div class="step-number">04</div>
<div class="step-content">
<h3>You Book & Pay Directly at the Clinic</h3>
<p>Once you select a clinic, we coordinate the appointment and share your pet's records. You pay the clinic directly — no billing through MedPup. Then you drive to your appointment.</p>
</div>
</div>

<div class="step">
<div class="step-number">05</div>
<div class="step-content">
<h3>MedPup Coordination Fee Is Paid Separately</h3>
<p>Our flat coordination fee is invoiced after appointment confirmation: $25–$50 for spay/neuter, $50–$100 for dental or soft-tissue surgery. Separate from the clinic bill.</p>
</div>
</div>

<div class="step">
<div class="step-number">06</div>
<div class="step-content">
<h3>Post-Op Follow-Up</h3>
<p>Your U.S. primary vet handles all post-operative rechecks. We provide them a complete surgical report and remain available for questions through recovery.</p>
</div>
</div>
