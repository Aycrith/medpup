## Frequently Asked Questions

### General

**Is this real?**
Yes. MedPup is a Pinellas County-based coordination service that connects pet owners with the lowest-cost licensed veterinary clinic within driving distance. We do not practice medicine; we coordinate appointments, confirm prices, and share records.

**Do you provide veterinary care?**
No. MedPup is a coordination service. All medical care is provided by independently licensed veterinarians at accredited partner clinics.

**Is the quality really the same?**
Yes. Our Phase 1 partner clinics — **Harmony Vet Care** (Oldsmar, 20 min), **ASPCA Community Veterinary Clinic** (Liberty City, Miami), and **Good Care Animal Clinic** (Hialeah) — are fully licensed Florida clinics. Harmony Vet Care is a non-profit with published pricing. ASPCA CVC is a nonprofit community clinic. Good Care is a 5-star Yelp-rated general practice. All use AVMA-recognized protocols.

**How much does this save me?**
Our verified Phase 1 pricing shows confirmed savings of **80-95% on spay/neuter** (ASPCA CVC: $40-$80 vs. ~$600-$1,200 U.S. average) and **83-94% on dental** (Good Care: $285-$595 vs. ~$1,500-$2,500 U.S. average).

**Is travel required?**
Yes — approximately 20 minutes to Harmony Vet Care (Oldsmar) or 1 hour to ASPCA CVC (Miami area) or Good Care (Hialeah). No customs, no APHIS paperwork, no ferry or flight. Just a direct car drive to a U.S. licensed clinic.

**Why are veterinary costs so high in the U.S.?**
Veterinary costs have risen 47% since 2020 — nearly double the general inflation rate over the same period (BLS data). This is driven by corporate consolidation, rising drug costs, and advanced diagnostic equipment costs passed to pet owners.

**What is the MedPup All-In Cost Guarantee?**
MedPup confirms the complete out-the-door cost before you book — including blood work, medications, X-rays, and other mandatory add-ons that clinics never publish in their marketing prices. If the final bill exceeds our confirmed range, we cover the excess or find you a backup clinic at no extra cost.

**Is this a nonprofit?**
No. MedPup is a for-profit business. Our coordination fee is $25-$100 per client — we earn our revenue through service, not through clinic kickbacks or percentage splits.

### Cost & Pricing

**What does the MedPup fee actually cover?**
The flat coordination fee covers: realistic cost calculation, clinic price verification, appointment reservation, guarantee administration, and records coordination. It's paid separately — the clinic's bill goes directly to the clinic.

**How much is the coordination fee?**
$25-$50 for spay/neuter appointments. $50-$100 for dental, surgery, or complex procedures. Flat fee, no percentage, no hidden charges.

**What if I can't afford the coordination fee?**
Contact us. We work with clients on a case-by-case basis. No one should forego necessary vet care because of a coordination fee.

**Do you offer payment plans for the coordination fee?**
Not yet. We're adding ACH/card payment for our coordination fee in the coming weeks. For now, payment is due at booking.

**Is CareCredit accepted?**
At partner clinics directly — some accept CareCredit, some are cash-only. We clarify this during your consultation so there are no surprises.

**What if the clinic charges more than quoted?**
We take responsibility. If the final bill exceeds our confirmed all-in range, we find you a backup clinic and cover the difference up to the cost of your coordination fee. No surprises, no disputes.

**How do you determine the all-in cost?**
We call each partner clinic with your pet's breed, weight, and diagnosis. We ask for the complete out-the-door quote including blood work, anesthesia, intraoperative monitoring, medications, and follow-up. We document this in writing before your appointment.

**Do I pay the clinic directly?**
Yes. The clinic's bill is paid directly at the clinic. MedPup's coordination fee is invoiced separately.

**What payment methods do clinics accept?**
ASPCA CVC is cash-only for the clinic fee. Good Care Animal Clinic accepts card and cash. Harmony Vet Care accepts card and CareCredit. We confirm payment options during consultation.

**How do I know the all-in price is accurate?**
We've verified pricing by reading actual client final bills from Reddit and vet forums. A "$40 spay" becomes $100-$240 after mandatory add-ons. A "$285 dental" becomes $895-$3,710. Our all-in ranges are based on real final bills, not marketing numbers.

### Booking & Process

**What do I need to get started?**
A diagnosis from your veterinarian confirming the recommended procedure. Optional: your pet's medical records and any imaging. We'll handle the rest.

**How do I book?**
Fill out the contact form or call for a free 15-minute consultation. We'll confirm the diagnosis, explain the pricing options, and book your appointment.

**What's your appointment cancellation policy?**
Cancellation 48+ hours before the appointment is free. Cancel within 48 hours and the coordination fee is forfeited. Clinic cancellations are handled directly with the clinic.

**How long does the intake process take?**
The initial consultation takes 15-20 minutes. We confirm pricing within 24 hours. Appointment is typically scheduled within 1-2 weeks depending on clinic availability.

**Do I need a vet referral?**
No. You need a confirmed diagnosis from any licensed veterinarian. If you don't have a regular vet, we can help you get a diagnosis first.

**Can I book for my cat?**
Yes. We coordinate care for both dogs and cats. Our current partner clinics handle both species.

**Do you handle emergency cases?**
No. MedPup is for non-emergency, elective, and scheduled procedures. If your pet needs emergency care, call your nearest emergency vet immediately.

**What if my pet needs multiple procedures?**
We can coordinate multiple procedures in a single trip. We'll confirm the combined all-in cost including any additional anesthesia or monitoring time.

### Safety & Quality

**Is the quality really the same as my regular vet?**
Yes — in some cases better. ASPCA CVC is operated by one of the largest animal welfare organizations in the world. Harmony Vet Care follows AVMA guidelines. All our partners use modern anesthetic monitoring, sterile surgical protocols, and board-eligible or board-certified surgeons.

**What safety protocols do partner clinics use?**
Standard AVMA-recommended protocols: pre-anesthetic blood work, IV catheterization, isoflurane gas anesthesia, pulse oximetry, ECG monitoring, temperature monitoring, and supervised recovery.

**What if there's a complication?**
Your regular vet handles all post-op rechecks. The operating clinic manages immediate complications. MedPup is available 24/7 for coordination questions.

**Are the clinics clean and modern?**
We inspect partner clinics through client reviews and direct verification. ASPCA CVC is a purpose-built modern facility. Good Care is an established practice with 5-star ratings.

**What if my pet has a pre-existing condition?**
Disclose all pre-existing conditions during the consultation so we can confirm the partner clinic can safely handle your pet's case. Some conditions require specialist referral.

**Do you coordinate specialist (board-certified) procedures?**
For procedures requiring board-certified specialists (complex orthopedics, oncology, neurology), we discuss options including Phase 3 international pathways. These specialist procedures typically cost 60-80% less at international hubs.

### Financing & Insurance

**Does pet insurance cover MedPup coordination?**
Pet insurance typically covers the medical procedure at the clinic (subject to your policy), not the coordination fee. Check with your insurer. Only 4.6% of U.S. pet owners have insurance, so this applies to a small minority.

**Does MedPup accept CareCredit?**
Our coordination fee is separate from the clinic fee. For the clinic fee, check with the specific partner clinic. Some accept CareCredit, some are cash-only.

**What is the typical return on investment?**
For a spay, you pay $25-$50 in coordination fees and save $500-$1,100 vs. U.S. average. For dental, you pay $50-$100 and save $1,000-$2,000. The ROI on the MedPup fee is 10x-20x.

**Can I use my HSA/FSA for MedPup fees?**
Many HSA and FSA plans cover veterinary coordination services. Check with your plan administrator. We provide detailed receipts for reimbursement.

**Do you offer a satisfaction guarantee?**
Yes. If the partner clinic does not meet the confirmed pricing or quality standards, we cover the difference and find you an alternative. This is part of our All-In Cost Guarantee.
