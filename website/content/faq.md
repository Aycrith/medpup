## Frequently Asked Questions

### General

**Is this real?**
Yes. MedPup is a Pinellas County–based coordination service that connects pet owners with the lowest-cost licensed veterinary clinic within driving distance. We do not practice medicine; we coordinate appointments, confirm prices, and share records.

**Do you provide veterinary care?**
No. MedPup is a coordination service. All medical care is provided by independently licensed veterinarians at accredited partner clinics.

**Is the quality really the same?**
Yes. Our Phase 1 partner clinics — ASPCA Community Veterinary Clinic (Liberty City, Miami) and Good Care Animal Clinic (Hialeah) — are fully licensed Florida clinics. ASPCA CVC is a nonprofit community clinic. Good Care is a 5-star Yelp-rated general practice with surgical capabilities. Both use AVMA-recognized protocols.

**How much does this save me?**
Our verified Phase 1 pricing shows confirmed savings of **80–95% on spay/neuter** (ASPCA CVC: $40–$80 vs. ~$600–$1,200 U.S. average) and **83–94% on dental** (Good Care: $285–$595 vs. ~$1,500–$2,500 U.S. average).

**Is travel required?**
Yes — approximately 1 hour from Largo to either clinic. No customs, no APHIS paperwork, no ferry or flight. Just a direct car drive to a U.S. licensed clinic.

### Booking & Appointments

**What do I need to get started?**
A diagnosis from your veterinarian confirming the recommended procedure. Optional: your pet's medical records and any imaging. We'll handle the rest.

**How do I book?**
Fill out the contact form or call for a free 15-minute consultation. We'll confirm the diagnosis, explain the pricing options, and book your appointment.

**What payment methods do you accept?**
Our coordination fee is invoiced separately. The clinic's bill is paid directly at the clinic (cash, CareCredit, or card depending on clinic). We're adding ACH/card payment for our own coordination fee in the coming weeks.

**What's your appointment cancellation policy?**
Cancellation 48+ hours before the appointment is free. Cancel within 48 hours and the coordination fee is forfeited. Clinic cancellations are handled directly with the clinic.

### Follow-Up Care

**What about follow-up care?**
Your U.S. primary vet handles all post-op rechecks. We'll give you a complete surgical report and recovery instructions from the partner clinic to forward to your regular vet.

**What if something goes wrong after surgery?**
We're available 24 hours by phone for questions related to the coordination and referral. Medical emergencies are handled by the operating clinic first, then your U.S. vet follows up.

### Domestic vs. International (Phase 3 Reference)

**Do you still handle Cancún or Freeport trips?**
Not currently — Phase 1 is the domestic South Florida model only. Cancún was downgraded to Phase 3 due to margin analysis showing negative-to-thin returns at prior pricing. Freeport's ferry model remains viable for future expansion but is not live today. For clients who self-select for international surgical travel when domestic options are insufficient, Phase 3 options can be discussed.

**Can I use CareCredit at Phase 1 clinics?**
ASPCA CVC is cash-only at the clinic; we invoice separately by ACH/card. If CareCredit access is important for your situation, we can note this and look for the closest accepting partner in a future expansion phase.
