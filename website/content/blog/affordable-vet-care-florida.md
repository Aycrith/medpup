### How do these clinics offer lower prices?

It's not about cutting corners — it's about different business models:

- **ASPCA Community Veterinary Clinic** is a subsidized nonprofit clinic operated by the ASPCA. Its mission is affordability, not profit.
- **Harmony Vet Care** is a non-profit with published pricing and transparent fee schedules.
- **Good Care Animal Clinic** is a lower-overhead general practice that doesn't carry the real estate and equipment costs of corporate-owned chains.

All three use AVMA-recognized protocols for anesthesia, surgery, and recovery.
