## The Pharmacy Ferry

Freeport is your entry point to affordable veterinary care. A 3-hour passenger ferry from Fort Lauderdale takes you and your pet to a fully accredited, English-speaking clinic where the same procedures cost 50-80% less than U.S. prices.

**Why Freeport is our Phase 1 hub:**

- **3-hour ferry** from Fort Lauderdale (Balearia Caribbean) — no airports, no cargo holds
- **CareCredit accepted** directly at the partner clinic — use your existing financing
- **No USDA endorsement** required for health certificates — simpler paperwork
- **No screwworm certificate** needed — fewer regulatory hurdles
- **Hawksbill Creek Agreement** — the Freeport Free Trade Zone eliminates taxes until 2054
