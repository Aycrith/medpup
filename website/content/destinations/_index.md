## MedPup Destinations

MedPup is a **Pinellas-based** coordination service. Our Phase 1 focus is domestic South Florida clinics reachable by car — no travel, no APHIS, no customs.

### Phase 1 — Domestic (Active Now)

**[South Florida Clinics →](/pricing/)** — ASPCA Community Veterinary Clinic (Liberty City, Miami) and Good Care Animal Clinic (Hialeah). Both are ~1 hour from Largo with verified pricing. Flat coordination fee only.

---

### Phase 3 — International (Archived / Future Option)

The following destinations were researched in Phase 0 but are **not part of the current launch plan**. Cancún has confirmed negative-to-thin margins at the proposed $900 concierge fee. Freeport's ferry model is better but not yet validated for Pinellas market demand. These are kept here for future reference.

**[Cancún, Mexico →](/destinations/cancun/)** *(Phase 3 — Mexico)*
2–4 night surgical trip via group charter from Miami. Complex orthopedics (TPLO, IVDD), oncology, advanced diagnostics — at 60–80% less than U.S. prices overall. Margin analysis: negative to razor-thin at current pricing. Not prioritized in Phase 1.

**[Freeport, Grand Bahama →](/destinations/freeport/)** *(Phase 3 — Bahamas)*
Day-trip via 3-hour ferry from Fort Lauderdale. Dental, mass removal, spay/neuter — 50–80% less than U.S. prices. CareCredit accepted. Margin profile better than Cancún but Phase 1 domestic model has priority.
