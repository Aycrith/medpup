MedPup's dual-hub model gives you the best option for your pet's specific needs — from a quick day-trip for dental work to a short stay for advanced orthopedic surgery.

**[Freeport, Grand Bahama →](/destinations/freeport/)** — Day-trip via 3-hour ferry from Fort Lauderdale. Ideal for dental, mass removal, spay/neuter, and chronic medication pickup. CareCredit accepted.

**[Cancun, Mexico →](/destinations/cancun/)** — 2-4 night trip via group charter from Miami. Ideal for TPLO, IVDD/spinal surgery, oncology, BOAS correction, and advanced diagnostics (MRI/CT).
