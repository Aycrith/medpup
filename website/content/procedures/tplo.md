## TPLO Surgery in Cancun — Save 60-80%

A TPLO (Tibial Plateau Leveling Osteotomy) is the gold-standard surgery for repairing a torn cranial cruciate ligament (CCL) in dogs — the canine equivalent of an ACL tear in humans. In the U.S., this surgery typically costs **$5,000 to $8,000+**. Through MedPup's Cancun hub, the same procedure is performed by board-certified surgeons for **~$1,200**.

---

### The Procedure

A TPLO changes the angle of the tibial plateau to eliminate the need for the cruciate ligament, stabilizing the knee joint. It has a **90-95% success rate** and is the preferred surgical method for large, active, or working dogs.

**Recovery time:** 8-12 weeks to full function, with leash walks only for the first 6-8 weeks.
