MedPup coordinates surgery for a wide range of veterinary procedures. Each is performed by licensed, credentialed surgeons at our inspected partner clinics in Freeport, Grand Bahama and Cancun, Mexico.

**[TPLO (ACL Surgery) →](/procedures/tplo/)** — Anterior cruciate ligament repair. U.S. cost: $5,000-8,000. MedPup Cancun: ~$1,200.

**[Dental Surgery →](/procedures/dental/)** — Cleanings, extractions, and oral surgery. U.S. cost: $500-3,000. MedPup Freeport: ~$150-300.

**[Soft Tissue Surgery →](/procedures/oncology/)** — Mass removal, spay/neuter, cystotomy, and oncology. U.S. cost: $600-10,000. MedPup: 60-80% less.
