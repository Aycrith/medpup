MedPup coordinates surgery for a wide range of veterinary procedures. Each is performed by licensed, credentialed surgeons at our inspected partner clinics in **South Florida** — ASPCA Community Veterinary Clinic (Liberty City, Miami) and Good Care Animal Clinic (Hialeah).

**[Spay / Neuter →](/pricing/#spayneuter)** — ASPCA CVC Liberty City. U.S. cost: $600–$1,200. MedPup-verified: $40–$80 + $25–$50 coordination fee. **~80–95% savings.**

**[Dental + Extractions →](/pricing/#dental)** — Good Care Animal Clinic, Hialeah. U.S. cost: $1,500–$2,500. MedPup-verified: $285–$595 + $50–$100 coordination fee. **~83–94% savings.**

**[Mass Removal →](/pricing/#massremoval)** — Good Care Animal Clinic, Hialeah. U.S. cost: $1,500–$3,000. ~$450 clinic fee + $50–$100 coordination fee.

**[Soft Tissue Surgery →](/pricing/#surgery)** — Good Care Animal Clinic. U.S. cost: $1,000–$4,200. MedPup-verified clinic pricing confirmed.

> **Complex orthopedic procedures (TPLO, IVDD/spinal surgery, oncology requiring board-certified specialist)** — Phase 1 clinics do not cover these. Phase 3 international options (Cancún) are being retained for future reference, but are not part of the current domestically validated Phase 1 launch.
