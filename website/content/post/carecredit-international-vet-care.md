## How CareCredit Works

CareCredit is a healthcare credit card that offers promotional financing (typically 0% APR for 6, 12, 18, or 24 months) on qualifying purchases. It's accepted at over **285,000 enrolled provider locations**, primarily in the United States.

**The key limitation:** CareCredit is a closed, domestic-only network. It cannot be used at clinics in Mexico, and attempting to route payments through a U.S. intermediary violates CareCredit's provider agreement.

## What If I Don't Have CareCredit?

CareCredit requires a minimum credit score of approximately 600. If you don't qualify, options include:

- Applying with a co-signer
- Using a standard credit card
