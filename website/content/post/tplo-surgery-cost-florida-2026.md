## Why TPLO Surgery Is So Expensive in Florida

TPLO (Tibial Plateau Leveling Osteotomy) is a complex orthopedic procedure. The cost drivers include:

- **Specialized equipment** — TPLO requires custom orthopedic implants and plates
- **Board-certified surgeon fees** — typically $1,500–$3,000 of the total
- **Anesthesia and monitoring** — advanced protocols add $400–$800
- **Pre-surgical workup** — bloodwork, x-rays, and sometimes CT scans ($300–$700)
- **Hospitalization and aftercare** — overnight stays, bandage changes, pain management

Private specialty hospitals in Tampa, St. Petersburg, and Clearwater routinely charge at the high end of this range. Even non-profit and general practice clinics in Pinellas County charge $2,500–$4,500.

## Is TPLO the Only Option?

For medium-to-large breed dogs, TPLO remains the gold standard with the best long-term outcomes. Tibial Tuberosity Advancement (TTA) and lateral suture (extracapsular) are alternatives — typically $1,000–$2,000 less expensive — but may not be appropriate for all dogs.
