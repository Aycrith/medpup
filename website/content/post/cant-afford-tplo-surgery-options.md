You're not alone. Veterinary costs have risen **47.15% since 2020**, nearly double the rate of general inflation. And TPLO is one of the most common — and most expensive — orthopedic procedures for dogs.

---

## Why Is TPLO So Expensive in the U.S.?

Several factors drive the high cost:

- **Specialized equipment:** TPLO requires a titanium plate and screws, precision surgical instruments, and intraoperative X-rays
- **Board-certified surgeons:** Specialist orthopedic surgeons command higher fees
- **Overhead:** U.S. veterinary hospitals carry high facility, staffing, and liability costs
- **Geography:** Prices vary significantly by region, with the Northeast and West Coast being most expensive

But here's the critical fact: **the procedure itself isn't inherently a $5,000+ surgery.** Much of the cost is driven by U.S. market dynamics, not the complexity of the procedure.
