## Why We Started MedPup

Every week, veterinarians across Florida face the same heartbreaking conversation: a beloved pet needs surgery, but the family simply can't afford the $5,000+ specialist quote. Too often, the alternatives are limited — live with the pain, surrender the pet, or choose euthanasia.

We started MedPup because we believe no family should have to make that choice.

## The Reality of U.S. Veterinary Costs

Veterinary costs have risen **47% since 2020** — nearly double the rate of general inflation. Today:
- **53% of pet owners** say a $500-$2,500 vet bill is impossible to pay
- **Over 50%** have delayed or skipped necessary care due to cost
- Only **4.6% of households** have pet insurance

Meanwhile, the same board-certified surgeons, the same advanced techniques, and the same high-quality care are available at clinics just a short trip from Florida — at 50-80% less.

## How MedPup Works

MedPup is a veterinary logistics concierge. We organize safe, stress-free trips from Florida to accredited partner clinics in Freeport, Grand Bahama and Cancun, Mexico. We handle all the logistics — USDA paperwork, import permits, travel bookings, clinic scheduling — so you can focus on what matters: your pet's recovery.

Same surgeons. Same equipment. Same standards. A fraction of the cost.
