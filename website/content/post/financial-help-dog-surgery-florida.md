## Florida-Specific Resources

### University of Florida Veterinary Teaching Hospital
- **Location:** Gainesville, FL
- **Savings:** 25-50% vs. private specialty hospitals
- **Best for:** Complex orthopedics, neurology, oncology
- **Wait time:** 4-12 weeks for non-emergency
- **Contact:** (352) 392-2235

### Florida Animal Friend Spay/Neuter Grant
- **What:** Free or reduced-cost spay/neuter for Florida residents
- **Eligibility:** Based on income
- **Contact:** floridaanimalfriend.org

### Humane Society of Greater Miami Low-Cost Clinic
- **What:** Reduced-cost spay/neuter, dental, minor surgery
- **Contact:** humanesocietymiami.org

## National Grant Programs

| Organization | Grant Amount | Conditions | Apply At |
|-------------|-------------|------------|----------|
| The Pet Fund | Up to $5,000 | Non-emergency, must apply before treatment | thepetfund.com |
| RedRover Relief | Up to $2,000 | Emergency life-saving care | redrover.org |
| Frankie's Friends | Varies | Life-threatening conditions, income-based | frankiesfriends.org |
| Brown Dog Foundation | Up to $1,000 | Treatable conditions with good prognosis | browndogfoundation.com |
| Magic Bullet Fund | Varies | Dogs diagnosed with cancer | themagicbulletfund.org |
| Mosby Foundation | Up to $1,500 | Critical care, abused/injured animals | themosbyfoundation.org |

**Tips for grant applications:**
- Apply early — many have limited monthly funds
- Get a written estimate from your vet before applying
- Include a photo of your dog and a compelling story
- Some require a vet to submit on your behalf
- Be prepared to provide income documentation

## Financing Options Available to Florida Residents

### CareCredit
- 12 million+ cardholders nationwide
- Used at thousands of Florida veterinary clinics
- Promotional 0% APR periods for 6-24 months
- Apply online at carecredit.com

### Scratchpay
- Higher approval rate than CareCredit
- Monthly payments as low as $49
- Designed specifically for vet care
- Apply at scratchpay.com
