# ASPCA CVC Liberty City — Outreach Script

**URL:** https://www.aspca.org/miami-initiative/community-veterinary-clinic  
**Verified price:** Spay/neuter $40–$80  
**Distance from Largo:** 1 hr (95 km)  
**Goal:** Ask if they accept external client referrals and what the lead time is for appointments.

---

## Call / Email Script (90 seconds)

> "Hi, I'm Cameron in Largo. I run MedPup, a Pinellas County-based service that helps pet owners find affordable veterinary care. I found your clinic through the ASPCA Community Medicine program — your spay/neuter pricing of $40–$80 is the best I've found in Florida, and I'd like to ask if you'd be open to an arrangement where I route clients to you."

**The Ask:**
1. "Do you currently accept client referrals from outside your immediate area?"
2. "What's your appointment lead time — same week, or longer?"
3. "Do you have a maximum number of referrals you can absorb per week?"

**If they ask what I charge:**  
"I charge a flat coordination fee to the client — $25 for spay/neuter, $50 for dental. The clinic bills the client directly. I never markup the procedure."

**If they ask about pricing verification:**  
"I sourced your pricing from your ASPCA webpage and public materials — the $40–$80 spay range. I want to confirm those numbers are current before I route anyone."
