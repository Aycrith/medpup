## Core Value Proposition to Communicate

ASPCA CVC publishes spay/neuter at $40–$80. That's real — and it's excellent. But here's what your clients experience when they show up:

- **Blood work is required** — +$60–$120 added at check-in
- **Pain meds are mandatory** — +$30–$80
- **E-collar and recovery supplies** — +$15–$30
- **Anesthesia tier upgrade if needed** — +$50–$200
- **Post-op recheck at their own vet** — +$40–$80
- **Post-op complications that get caught at surgery** — +$100–$300+

A client who sees $40 on your website leaves paying $280–$350. That's a **bill shock** we prevent. We want to route clients who accept those add-ons — but want them **confirmed in writing before they drive.**
