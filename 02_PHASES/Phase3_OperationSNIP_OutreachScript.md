## Call Script (2 minutes)

**Q1:** "Do you currently take client referrals, or do you operate your own clinic?"

## Decision Tree

| Their answer | Action |
|---|---|
| They route to low-cost clinics already | **Probe partnership** — "We have clients who need appointments. Would you be open to us sending you referrals?" |
| They have their own clinic, NOT low-cost | **Mark as competitor** — record their prices, identify their niche, leave open for future partnership |
| They are unclear / don't pick up | **Leave voicemail** (template below) |
| They ask what MedPup charges | "We charge a coordinated flat fee per appointment — clients pay the clinic directly." (Do not quote a number) |
