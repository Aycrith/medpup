# Humane Society of Tampa Bay — Outreach Script

**URL:** humanesocietytampa.org  
**Verified price (in DB):** Microchip $35  
**Distance from Largo:** 30 min  
**Goal:** Get spay/neuter and dental pricing. Ask if they accept external referrals. If spay pricing is competitive with ASPCA CVC, they become a local Pinellas anchor.

## Call Script (2 minutes)

> "Hi, I'm Cameron in Largo — I run MedPup, a service that helps Pinellas pet owners find affordable vet care. I'm building a network of low-cost referral partners and HSTB came up as the leading humane society option in the Tampa Bay area. I had two quick questions."

**Q1:** "Do you publish spay/neuter pricing on your website or by phone? What's the range for a 60-pound dog?"

**Q2:** "Do you accept client inquiries from outside your immediate service area or do you restrict appointments to Hillsborough residents?"

**Q3 (if time):** "Do you offer dental cleanings, and at what price point?"
