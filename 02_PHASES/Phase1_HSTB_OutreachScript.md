# Humane Society of Tampa Bay — Outreach Script (All-In Cost Guarantee framing)

**URL:** humanesocietytampa.org  
**Verified price (in DB):** Microchip $35  
**Distance from Largo:** 30 min  
**Goal:** Get spay/neuter and dental pricing with all-in add-ons included. Ask if they accept external referrals. If spay pricing is competitive with ASPCA CVC, HSTB becomes a local Pinellas anchor.

---

## Core Value Proposition to Communicate

HSTB publishes a microchip at $35. That tells us their price floor is very good.
But here's what a pet owner who calls Google's number for a spay quote gets back:

- **Marketing price:** $60–$100 (typical humane society spay bargain)
- **The add-ons that arrive at checkout:**
  - Pre-op blood work — +$60–$120
  - Pain medication (take-home) — +$30–$80
  - E-collar / recovery cone — +$15–$30
  - Post-op recheck at their vet — +$40–$80

A client who calls expecting $60 leaves paying $250+. That's the bill shock we prevent.

**MedPup's pitch:** We'll route HSTB cross-referrals from Pinellas County. Our service confirms the all-in cost (marketing price + add-ons listed transparently) before the client books. If final bill exceeds our confirmed range, MedPup covers the excess or finds a backup clinic. That's nothing HSTB could offer alone — but together, it means HSTB gets qualified, no-surprise clients who show up ready and willing.

---

## Call Script (2 minutes)

> "Hi, I'm Cameron in Largo — I run MedPup, a service that helps Pinellas pet owners find affordable vet care with all-in cost confirmed before arrival. I'm building a network of affordable referral partners in the Tampa Bay area and HSTB came up as the most trusted option locally. I had two quick questions, if you have 30 seconds."

**Q1:** "Do you publish spay/neuter pricing on your website or by phone? What's the range for a 60-pound dog? And specifically — does that number include blood work and pain medication upfront, or are those added items?"

**Q2:** "Do you accept client inquiries from outside your immediate service area, or do you restrict appointments to Hillsborough residents? We'd be sending clients from Pinellas County specifically."

**Q3 (if time):** "Do you offer dental cleanings, and at what price point? Is that the all-in number, or are extractions and X-rays billed separately?"
