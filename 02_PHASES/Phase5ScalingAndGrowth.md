## 3. Concierge Recruitment & Training

Once trips are weekly, you cannot be the sole concierge. Hire a 1099 contractor.

### Job Description (Short)

```
MEDPUP VETERINARY CONCIERGE (1099 Contractor)

We're looking for a reliable, pet‑loving professional to accompany Florida dog owners on day‑trips to Freeport, Bahamas. You'll handle all travel logistics, paperwork verification, and on‑site support at our partner veterinary clinic.

Requirements:
- Available Saturdays (6 AM – 9 PM)
- Valid U.S. passport
- Experience handling dogs of all sizes and temperaments
- Calm under pressure; strong communication skills
- Veterinary technician, vet assistant, or experienced pet sitter preferred
- Bilingual (English/Spanish) a plus

Pay: $200 per trip + ferry fare + meals. 1‑2 trips per month to start.

Apply: [Email] with "MedPup Concierge" in subject.
```

### Training Protocol

1. **Shadow Trip:** Trainee shadows you on one full trip as an observer.
2. **Assisted Trip:** Trainee runs the trip with you observing and intervening only if necessary.
3. **Solo Trip:** Trainee runs the trip alone. You're on‑call by phone.
4. **Certification:** After 2 successful solo trips with positive client feedback, they're fully certified.

**Provide each trainee with:**
- Printed and laminated Pre‑Flight Checklist
- Access to the CRM (view‑only client trip details)
- MedPup Go‑Bag (duplicate yours)
- 24‑hour emergency line number
