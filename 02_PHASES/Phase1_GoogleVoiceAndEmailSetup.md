# Google Voice + Business Email Setup — 30-Minute Guide

---

## Google Voice (Free)

1. Go to **voice.google.com** — must use Chrome
2. Sign in with your Gmail account
3. Click **"Get Google Voice"** or **"Choose a number"**
4. Search for **727 area code** (Pinellas County)
5. Pick any available number
6. Verify with your existing cell phone number
7. Set up voicemail greeting (mention MedPup)
8. Forward calls to your cell — done

**Gotcha:** Google Voice only works on the web if you call *from* the web. The app forwards to your cell. For a dedicated business line feel, the $6/mo Google Voice Workspace plan is worth it.

## Business Email — Two Options

### A. Google Workspace ($6/month, recommended)
1. Go to **workspace.google.com**
2. Create `hello@medpup.com` — requires domain ownership
3. If you don't own a domain yet: **skip this for now**, use Gmail
4. Once you have a domain (e.g. `medpup.com`), setup takes 30 minutes

### B. Gmail (Free, for now)
Create: `medpup.pinellas@gmail.com` — forwarding only until domain is owned
