## 2. Negotiation Talking Points (Phone/In‑Person)

**Goal:** Secure a 10‑15% discount for MedPup referrals in exchange for volume and zero marketing cost to the clinic.

| MedPup’s Value Add | Ask in Return |
|---------------------|---------------|
| We bring 20–50 pre‑booked patients per year, fully vetted, paperwork complete. | 10‑15% off published surgery rates. |
| We handle all communication, translation, and payment issues. | Transparent price list for the most common procedures. |
| We provide a 24/7 concierge on‑site during the trip, reducing staff burden. | Willingness to sign a simple MOU (Memorandum of Understanding). |
| We’ll feature your clinic prominently on our website and social media (free marketing). | Photo consent and a brief bio of the lead surgeon. |
| We’ll pay a small “facility coordination fee” per trip if a discount isn’t possible. | Clear scheduling windows (e.g., we can book the surgical suite for a 4‑hour block). |

**Fallback position:** If the clinic won’t discount, ask for a “preferred appointment time” (e.g., first surgery slot of the day) so the concierge can manage recovery timing better.
