## FRESH — Idiom Puns (Like FurGood)

### Fur Deal
**"For real" + "deal" (good value).**
The only new pun I haven't shown you. "Fur real" (for real) + "it's a good deal" (value). Double meaning: authenticity AND affordability. Works as a statement ("Fur Deal — the real deal for your pet"). Short. Warm. Confident.
**Tagline:** *"The fur deal. For real."*

### FurKeeps
**"For keeps"** — permanently, forever yours. 
A pun on the phrase "for keeps" meaning something is yours forever. Your pet's health, kept. The pun is satisfying without being silly.
**Tagline:** *"Health, fur keeps."*

### KeepPaws / PawsKept
**"Paws kept"** — similar to above but with paws.

## FRESH — Single Word Names

These are names that don't EXPLAIN the business. They're evocative, not descriptive — like Rover, Chewy, Warby Parker.

| Name | Why | Vibe |
|------|-----|------|
| **Pax** | Peace + sounds like "paws" + sounds like "packs" (bags). 3 letters. Modern. "Pax Pet Care." | Calm, short, brandable |
| **Mend** | Heal, repair. Already a word. "Your pet, mended." Positive outcome. | Medical + warm |
| **Prime** | Prime = best + first-rate + primary. "Prime Pet." Short, confident, premium | Premium, confident |
| **Fair** | Fair pricing + fair treatment + "to the fair" (gathering, travel). "Fair Pet Care." | Honest, warm |
| **Whole** | Whole health + "make whole." "Whole Pet." Warm but generic. | Complete, warm |
| **Worth** | "Your pet is worth it" + worth the cost. "Worth Pet Care." | Emotional + value |
