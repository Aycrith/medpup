### The Coordination Fee Model

**Spay/neuter routing (easiest start):**
- You find clients → route to ASPCA CVC ($80 dog spay) or Paws 2 Help ($120 dog spay)
- Charge client: $25-50 flat coordination fee (booking support, reminders, price comparison)
- Savings to client: 65-85% even WITH your fee

**Dental routing (higher margin):**
- You find clients → route to Good Care Animal Clinic ($250-$350 dental)
- Charge client: $50-100 coordination fee
- Savings to client: still 70-85% vs full-service

### Legal Structure (From Perplexity Research)

```
You (Founder)
    |
    v
Florida Referral & Operations LLC  <-- You own this
    |
    ├── Website / Intake System
    ├── Client coordination & scheduling
    ├── Flat fee (not per-procedure commission)
    └── Marketing & lead generation
            |
            v
Low-Cost Partner Clinics  <-- Independently owned
    └── Licensed Veterinarian controls all medical decisions
```

**Safe compensation models:**
- Flat coordination fee per client
- Monthly software/admin subscription
- Flat concierge fee
- NEVER per-procedure revenue share

## 4. What Each Phase Looks Like

### Phase 1: Referral Coordination ($0 capital)
**Months 1-3:**
- Route spay/neuter leads to 5 partner clinics
- Build client base of 20-50 routed cases/month
- Prove you can generate demand
- Collect pricing data on dental/mass removal from partners
- Revenue: $500-$2,500/month in coordination fees

### Phase 2: Membership + Concierge (low capital)
**Months 3-6:**
- Add $9-19/month membership tier (priority booking, reminders, price routing)
- Add concierge transport option (Texas border, GA)
- Scale to 100+ routed cases/month
- Revenue: $2,000-$5,000/month
- Use data to pitch DVM cofounder

### Phase 3: Physical Clinic (requires capital/investor)
**Months 6-12:**
- Recruit DVM as medical director
- Open limited-scope surgical/dental center
- MSO structure: you own operations, doctor owns medicine
- Target: Dr. Kelly's pricing model in an underserved Miami location
- Capital needed: $50K-$250K for mobile launch, $500K+ for fixed facility
