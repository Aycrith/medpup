## 6. Q&A Entries

Add these questions and answers to the GBP Q&A section:

**Q: How much can I save through MedPup?**
A: Most clients save 50-80% compared to U.S. prices. For example, a TPLO that costs $5,000-$8,000 in the U.S. costs approximately $2,700 total through our Cancun program. A dental with extractions costing $1,500-$3,000 in the U.S. costs approximately $1,200 through our Freeport program.

**Q: Do I travel with my dog?**
A: For Freeport day-trips, you're welcome to join — the 3-hour ferry is pet-friendly and comfortable. For Cancun surgical trips, you can join the group charter or your dog travels with our trained concierge.

**Q: Can I use CareCredit?**
A: Yes! CareCredit is accepted directly at our Freeport, Bahamas partner clinic. You can use your existing CareCredit card for the surgical fees.

**Q: Will my regular vet still be involved?**
A: Absolutely. Your U.S. veterinarian provides the initial diagnosis and health certificate, and handles the 14-day post-operative recheck. We send them a complete surgical report.

**Q: Is the quality the same as the U.S.?**
A: Yes. Our partner clinics employ board-certified surgeons, use WHO Surgical Safety Checklists, and have advanced equipment comparable to top U.S. specialty hospitals. Every clinic is inspected in person before partnership.

**Q: Is it legal?**
A: Yes. Over 36,000 dogs crossed from the U.S. into Mexico for care in 2025 alone. USDA APHIS has clear regulations for pet re-entry. We handle 100% of the paperwork.

**Q: How long is a trip?**
A: Freeport is a same-day trip (depart 7 AM, return 8 PM). Cancun is 4 days, 3 nights (Thursday through Sunday).

**Q: What procedures do you offer?**
A: We offer dental surgery, mass removal, spay/neuter, TPLO/ACL surgery, IVDD/spinal surgery, BOAS/airway correction, oncology surgery, and advanced diagnostics (MRI/CT).

**Q: Do you accept pet insurance?**
A: Some pet insurance providers cover procedures performed internationally. We recommend contacting your provider before booking. We provide complete surgical reports and invoices for claim submission.

**Q: What if something goes wrong?**
A: Our partner clinics maintain full emergency protocols. An on-site concierge is present throughout all procedures. You receive a 24-hour emergency hotline for any post-trip concerns.
