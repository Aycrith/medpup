## Scoring Methodology

Each name scored 1-10 across 11 criteria. Weighted total = final score.

| Professionalism | 9 | Must work in medical/veterinary context |
| Memorability | 9 | Word of mouth is primary marketing |

## Recommendation

**VetFlow** scores highest across all criteria: professional enough for veterinary partners, modern enough for pet owners, no negative associations, available as vetflow.vet right now, brandable visual identity (flow = water = smooth = ferry route), and the .com is parked (negotiable later).

**Furway** is the strongest alternative if you want shorter/warmer over professional. 6 letters, furway.vet available, and the shortest domain on the list.

Both are ready to register at any domain registrar (Porkbun, Namecheap, Cloudflare — .vet TLD, ~$20-30/year).
