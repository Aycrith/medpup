## STEP 1: Deploy a Simple Website (Day 1 – 2 hours)

**Do not overcomplicate this.** You need a single page that explains what you do and collects inquiries.

### Option A: Carrd.co (Fastest – 60 minutes)
1. Go to carrd.co, sign up (free or $9/year Pro).
2. Choose a single‑page template.
3. Add this content:
   - **Headline:** "Can't Afford Your Dog's Surgery? Save 60–80% with MedPup."
   - **Body:** "We coordinate day‑trips from Fort Lauderdale to an accredited Freeport, Bahamas clinic. Same world‑class surgery, a fraction of the cost. We handle all paperwork, ferry tickets, and stay with your dog the entire day."
   - **Bullet points:** Dental from $400 • Mass removal from $500 • Spay/Neuter from $150 • CareCredit accepted
   - **Call to action button:** "Get a Free Consultation" → links to a Google Form or Calendly.
   - **Trust elements:** "Licensed & inspected clinic partner • USDA paperwork handled • Concierge by your side"
4. Publish to a free `.carrd.co` subdomain or connect your `medpup.com` domain.
5. Add the Google Form (Step 1b).

### Option B: Hugo + GitHub Pages (90 minutes – if you prefer the full site)
Follow the deployment instructions in `Phase2DigitalInfrastructure.md`. Just get the Home page and the intake form live. All other pages can come later.

### The Intake Form (5 minutes)
Create a free Google Form with these fields:
- Owner name
- Email
- Phone
- Pet name, breed, age, weight
- Procedure needed (checkboxes: Dental, Mass Removal, Spay/Neuter, Other)
- Estimated U.S. quote ($)
- How did you hear about us?

Set the form to email you on every submission. That's your CRM until EspoCRM is set up.

## STEP 5: Book Consultations & Close (Days 4–7 – Ongoing)

When inquiries come in (from the form, ads, or vets):

1. **Reply within 2 hours.** Use this template:
```
Hi [Name],

Thanks for reaching out about [Pet Name]! I'd love to help you save on [procedure].

Let's schedule a quick 15‑minute call to go over how it works, answer your questions, and see if a MedPup trip is right for you.

I'm available [Day] at [Time] or [Day] at [Time]. What works best?

Best,
[Your Name]
```

2. **On the call:**
   - Listen to their story. Empathize.
   - Explain the process in 3 sentences: "We take the 7 AM ferry from Fort Lauderdale. You and your dog are with a personal concierge all day. The clinic is fully inspected and accepts CareCredit."
   - Answer the 3 fears: safety, quality, legality.
   - State the price clearly: "$285 concierge fee + the clinic's surgery fee (we'll give you an exact quote)."
   - Ask: "Would you like me to send you the exact clinic quote and get you booked?"

3. **After the call:**
   - Send the Client Service Agreement (from `Phase3ClientOnboardingKit.md`).
   - Use Invoice Ninja (free) to send the $285 invoice.
   - Once paid, send the Pre‑Trip Checklist.
   - Mark them as "Booked" in your tracker.

You do not need a CRM, automation, a full website, or a social media following to run this trip. You need:
- A clinic that says yes.
