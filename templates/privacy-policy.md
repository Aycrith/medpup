# Privacy Policy

**MedPup Veterinary Concierge, LLC** (dba "MedPup")  
Last Updated: May 20, 2026

---

## 1. Who We Are

MedPup Veterinary Concierge, LLC ("MedPup," "we," "us," or "our") is a Florida-based veterinary coordination service. We connect pet owners with low-cost veterinary clinics. We do not practice veterinary medicine. Our website is located at **medpup.com** (the "Site").

**Registered Address:** Pinellas County, Florida

---

## 2. Scope of This Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or use our coordination services. It applies to all users of our Site and services ("you" or "your").

This policy complies with:
- **California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA)** — for California residents
- **Florida Information Protection Act (FIPA)** — as a Florida business
- **General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)** — for users in the European Economic Area

---

## 3. Information We Collect

### 3.1 Information You Provide to Us

We collect information you voluntarily provide when you:

- Fill out an intake form or welcome packet
- Create an account
- Request our coordination services
- Contact us via email, phone, or web form
- Sign up for newsletters or marketing communications

This information may include:

| Category | Examples |
|---|---|
| **Contact Information** | Full name, email address, phone number, mailing address, ZIP code |
| **Pet Information** | Pet name, species, breed, weight, age, medical procedure needed |
| **Veterinary Details** | U.S. veterinarian diagnosis, cost quote received |
| **Referral Source** | How you heard about MedPup |
| **Communication Records** | Emails, chat messages, call recordings (with notice) |
| **Payment Information** | Credit/debit card details (processed via third-party payment processor; we do not store full card numbers) |

### 3.2 Information Collected Automatically

When you visit our Site, we automatically collect:

- **Device & Browser Info:** IP address, browser type, operating system, device type
- **Usage Data:** Pages viewed, time spent, links clicked, referral URLs
- **Cookies & Tracking:** See Section 7 below

### 3.3 Information from Third Parties

We may receive information from:
- Veterinary clinics you authorize us to contact
- Payment processors (limited to transaction confirmations)
- Analytics providers (e.g., Google Analytics)

## 5. How We Share Your Information

We share your information only in the following circumstances:

### 5.1 With Veterinary Clinics

We share your name, pet information, and relevant veterinary details with the low-cost clinic where your appointment is scheduled. This is necessary to coordinate your pet's care.

### 5.2 Service Providers

We engage trusted third-party service providers who process data on our behalf:

- Payment processors (Stripe, PayPal, or similar)
- Email communication platforms
- Website hosting and analytics services
- Customer relationship management (CRM) tools

All service providers are contractually bound to protect your data and may only use it for the purposes we specify.

### 5.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in response to valid legal requests (e.g., court orders, subpoenas).

### 5.4 Business Transfers

In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction. We will notify you via email and/or a prominent notice on our Site.

### 5.5 With Your Consent

We may share your information for any other purpose disclosed to you at the time of collection, with your consent.

## 8. Your Rights & Choices

### 8.1 California Residents (CCPA Rights)

If you are a California resident, you have the following rights:

| Right | Description |
|---|---|
| **Right to Know** | Request disclosure of the categories and specific pieces of personal information we have collected about you in the past 12 months. |
| **Right to Delete** | Request deletion of personal information we have collected from you, subject to certain exceptions. |
| **Right to Correct** | Request correction of inaccurate personal information. |
| **Right to Opt Out** | Opt out of the "sale" or "sharing" of your personal information for cross-context behavioral advertising. We do not currently sell personal information. |
| **Right to Non-Discrimination** | We will not discriminate against you for exercising any of your CCPA rights. |

**To exercise your CCPA rights**, submit a verifiable consumer request by:
- Email: **privacy@medpup.com**
- Phone: **(727) 555-MEDPUP**

We will confirm receipt within 10 business days and respond substantively within 45 days (up to 90 days with notice).

**Verification:** We will ask you to verify your identity by matching at least two data points we have on file before processing your request.

**Authorized Agent:** You may designate an authorized agent to make a request on your behalf. We will require proof of written authorization.

**Sensitive Personal Information:** We do not collect or process "sensitive personal information" (as defined by the CCPA) for purposes other than providing our services.

### 8.2 Florida Residents (FIPA)

Under the Florida Information Protection Act, we are required to notify you in the event of a data breach involving your personal information. Notifications will be made without unreasonable delay.

### 8.3 EEA Residents (GDPR Rights)

If you are located in the European Economic Area, you have the following rights:

- **Right of Access** — Request a copy of your personal data
- **Right to Rectification** — Correct inaccurate data
- **Right to Erasure** ("Right to be Forgotten") — Request deletion
- **Right to Restrict Processing** — Limit how we use your data
- **Right to Data Portability** — Receive your data in a machine-readable format
- **Right to Object** — Object to processing based on legitimate interests or direct marketing

**To exercise your GDPR rights**, contact us at **privacy@medpup.com**.

We rely on the following lawful bases for processing: performance of a contract, legitimate interests, consent (where required), and legal obligation.

**International Transfers:** If we transfer your data outside the EEA, we ensure appropriate safeguards are in place (e.g., Standard Contractual Clauses).

**Supervisory Authority:** You have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

## 9. Data Security

We implement reasonable and appropriate security measures to protect your personal information, including:

- **Encryption:** SSL/TLS for data in transit; AES-256 for data at rest where feasible
- **Access Controls:** Role-based access, least-privilege principle
- **Vendor Due Diligence:** All third-party service providers are vetted for security
- **Incident Response Plan:** Documented breach detection, response, and notification procedures

**No method of electronic storage or transmission is 100% secure.** We cannot guarantee absolute security.

## 10. Children's Privacy

If we become aware that a child has provided us with personal data, we will take steps to delete it promptly. Please contact us if you believe a child has submitted data to us.
