### 1. PURPOSE

This Memorandum of Understanding (MOU) establishes a framework for cooperation between MedPup and the Clinic to provide veterinary surgical and medical services to pet owners referred by MedPup from the United States.

### 2. SERVICES PROVIDED BY CLINIC

The Clinic agrees to:
- Provide veterinary surgical and dental procedures as listed in the attached Price Schedule (Schedule A)
- Perform procedures in a timely manner as scheduled with MedPup
- Provide a written post-operative discharge summary for each MedPup-referred patient
- Maintain a clean, safe, and properly equipped surgical facility
- Ensure all veterinary staff are properly licensed and credentialed

### 3. SERVICES PROVIDED BY MEDPUP

MedPup agrees to:
- Refer pre-qualified clients to the Clinic for scheduled procedures
- Manage all U.S. and destination-country regulatory documentation
- Coordinate travel logistics (transportation, permits, scheduling)
- Provide an on-site concierge during group trip days
- Handle all U.S.-side client communication and billing for MedPup's concierge services

### 4. FINANCIAL TERMS

- **Payment:** Pet owner pays Clinic directly at time of service via cash, credit card, or CareCredit (where applicable)
- **Pricing:** Clinic agrees to provide MedPup with a published price list (Schedule A) and to honor those prices for MedPup-referred patients
- **Volume Pricing:** [Optional: If volume threshold is met, Clinic agrees to \_\_\_% discount on published rates]
- **MedPup Compensation:** MedPup does not receive any portion of Clinic's surgical fees. MedPup's compensation comes solely from client concierge fees

### 5. BOOKING AND SCHEDULING

- MedPup will provide at least 14 days' notice for scheduled group trips
- For each trip, MedPup will specify: number of patients, procedure types, patient weights, and any special medical needs
- Clinic will confirm availability within 48 hours
- Emergency or urgent cases may be accommodated on a best-effort basis

### 6. DOCUMENTATION AND COMMUNICATION

- Clinic will provide a complete surgical record for each patient within 24 hours of procedure completion
- Surgical records will include: procedure performed, findings, medications administered, discharge instructions, and follow-up recommendations
- Clinic surgeon will be available for a brief phone or video handoff with the patient's U.S. primary veterinarian upon request

### 7. LIABILITY

- The Clinic maintains professional liability insurance covering all veterinary services provided
- MedPup maintains general business liability insurance covering its non-clinical coordination services
- Each party is solely responsible for claims arising from its own acts or omissions
- Nothing in this MOU creates a joint venture, partnership, or employer-employee relationship

### 8. CONFIDENTIALITY

Both parties agree to protect the confidentiality of client/patient information in accordance with applicable privacy laws.

### 9. TERM AND TERMINATION

- This MOU is effective as of the date above and continues until terminated
- Either party may terminate with 30 days' written notice
- Either party may terminate immediately for material breach
- MedPup client trips already scheduled at time of termination will be honored

### 10. DISCLAIMER

This MOU is a statement of intent and mutual understanding. It is not a legally binding contract unless explicitly executed as such by both parties.
