## Welcome! Here's How MedPup Works

Thank you for choosing **MedPup** to help coordinate your pet's veterinary care. We're glad you're here.

### What MedPup Does

**MedPup is a veterinary coordination service — we are NOT a veterinary clinic.**

MedPup saves you time and money by connecting you with low-cost veterinary clinics in the Pinellas County area. Think of us as your personal care coordinator:

- ✅ We research and match you with participating low-cost clinics
- ✅ We schedule your appointment
- ✅ We relay your pet's information to the clinic (with your permission)
- ✅ We provide you with a clear, upfront estimate of total costs

### What MedPup Does NOT Do

- ❌ We do NOT practice veterinary medicine
- ❌ We do NOT diagnose, prescribe, or treat medical conditions
- ❌ We do NOT employ veterinarians
- ❌ We do NOT operate any veterinary facility
- ❌ We cannot guarantee specific medical outcomes

**Your pet's medical care is always provided by licensed veterinarians at the clinic. You should always share your pet's complete medical history and follow the veterinarian's advice.**

## The All-In Cost Guarantee

**No surprises. Period.**

MedPup operates with a simple promise: we will tell you the **total estimated cost** — including both the clinic's fee and our coordination fee — **before** you commit to anything.

If the clinic's actual charge exceeds our quoted amount by more than 10% (excluding any additional services you authorize at the time of service, or unforeseen complications), we will refund the difference up to the full amount of our coordination fee.

**How it works:**

1. You tell us what procedure your pet needs and share your diagnosis and cost quote
2. We find a participating clinic and provide you with the all-in estimate
3. You choose to proceed (or not) — no pressure
4. If the clinic's final bill exceeds the estimate by more than 10%, we make it right

## Our Fee Structure

| Item | Amount |
|---|---|
| **MedPup Coordination Fee** | **$25 – $100 flat fee** (varies by complexity; disclosed upfront) |
| **Clinic Fee** | Billed directly by the clinic — varies by procedure and location |
| **Total You Pay** | MedPup fee + clinic fee (both disclosed before you commit) |

**What our fee covers:**
- Clinic research and matching
- Appointment scheduling
- Administrative support
- Communication facilitation with the clinic

**What our fee does NOT cover:**
- Veterinary procedures, exams, tests, or treatments
- Medications, vaccines, or supplies
- Emergency or after-hours care
- Transportation

**Payment:** We accept all major credit/debit cards. Clinic fees are paid directly to the clinic at the time of service.

## What to Expect

### Before Your Appointment

1. **Complete the Intake Form** (below) — tell us about you, your pet, and the procedure needed
2. **Share your vet diagnosis & quote** — we need your U.S. veterinarian's diagnosis and the cost quote you received (this helps us match you and honor our All-In Cost Guarantee)
3. **We'll contact you** within 1–2 business days with clinic options and your all-in estimate
4. **You confirm** — once you approve, we schedule your appointment
5. **We send confirmations** — you'll receive appointment details and clinic info

### Day of the Appointment

1. **Arrive on time** at the clinic we coordinated
2. **Check in** with the clinic's front desk — let them know you're with MedPup
3. **Bring your pet** and any required documents (vaccination records, ID, etc.)
4. **Pay the clinic directly** for their services
5. **The veterinarian** will examine and treat your pet

### After the Appointment

1. **Follow any post-procedure instructions** provided by the veterinarian
2. **Contact the clinic directly** for any follow-up care questions or concerns
3. **Contact MedPup** at support@medpup.com if you have questions about your coordination experience or if the final clinic bill exceeded our estimate by more than 10%
4. **Leave a review** and let us know how we did! Your feedback helps us improve
