### 1. SERVICES PROVIDED BY MEDPUP

MedPup Veterinary Concierge, LLC ("MedPup") agrees to provide the following coordination services for the Client and their Pet (the "Trip"):

- Booking of round-trip transportation (ferry or charter flight as applicable) from departure point in Florida to destination
- Coordination of required USDA APHIS health certificates and destination-country import permits
- Scheduling of surgical procedure(s) at the designated partner clinic (the "Clinic")
- On-site concierge accompaniment during the Trip
- Regulatory documentation management and compliance coordination
- General logistical support and communication facilitation between Client and Clinic

### 2. MEDPUP'S ROLE DISCLOSURE

MedPup is a **logistics and travel concierge, NOT a veterinary practice.**

MedPup does NOT:
- Diagnose medical conditions
- Perform surgery or medical procedures
- Prescribe medications
- Make clinical decisions
- Employ or supervise veterinarians

MedPup's role is strictly limited to non-clinical coordination: travel logistics, regulatory documentation, and on-site support.

### 3. INDEPENDENT VETERINARY SERVICES

All surgical and medical services are provided exclusively by the independently licensed veterinarians at the designated partner clinic (the "Treating Veterinarian").

The Client acknowledges that:
- The decision to proceed with surgery is made by the Client based on their own veterinary consultations
- MedPup has not diagnosed any condition, recommended any procedure, or guaranteed any outcome
- The Treating Veterinarian is solely responsible for the quality and outcome of all medical care
- MedPup makes no warranties, express or implied, regarding the outcome of any veterinary procedure
- The Client has been advised to consult their primary U.S. veterinarian regarding the suitability of the procedure and travel

### 4. INFORMED CONSENT

The Client acknowledges that any surgical procedure carries inherent risks, including but not limited to: adverse reaction to anesthesia, infection, bleeding, delayed healing, and in rare cases, death.

The Client confirms they have:
- Discussed the risks of the planned procedure(s) with the Treating Veterinarian
- Had the opportunity to ask questions and receive answers
- Received information about expected outcomes and recovery

The Client consents to the planned procedure(s) as described in the trip itinerary.

### 5. CLIENT RESPONSIBILITIES

The Client agrees to:
- Provide accurate and complete medical history, vaccination records, and current health status for the Pet
- Obtain any required pre-trip diagnostics (bloodwork, imaging) as recommended by the Clinic or MedPup
- Ensure the Pet is fasted (no food after midnight) on the Trip Date unless otherwise instructed by the Clinic
- Bring the original USDA-endorsed health certificate and rabies vaccination certificate on the Trip Date
- Follow all post-operative instructions provided by the Clinic
- Schedule and attend a follow-up examination with their primary U.S. veterinarian within 14 days of the Trip
- Promptly report any post-operative complications to the Clinic and to MedPup

### 6. TRAVEL AND LOGISTICS

MedPup will use commercially reasonable efforts to ensure smooth travel, but cannot guarantee against delays caused by weather, mechanical issues, regulatory changes, or other circumstances beyond its control.

The Client is responsible for ensuring their Pet meets all pre-travel health requirements as communicated by MedPup. Failure to provide required documentation may result in trip cancellation.

### 7. FINANCIAL TERMS

**Concierge Fee:** The Client agrees to pay MedPup the concierge fee specified above. This fee covers MedPup's non-clinical coordination services only. Due in full upon signing this Agreement.

**Surgical Fees:** All fees charged by the Treating Veterinarian/Clinic are the sole responsibility of the Client and shall be paid directly to the Clinic. MedPup is not a party to this transaction and does not mark up or receive any portion of surgical fees.

**Cancellation:**
- More than 7 days before Trip Date: Concierge fee refundable (less $50 processing fee)
- Within 7 days of Trip Date: Concierge fee non-refundable
- Ferry/charter tickets: Subject to carrier's cancellation policy
- Clinic surgical deposit (if any): Subject to Clinic's policy

### 8. RELEASE AND INDEMNIFICATION

To the fullest extent permitted by law, the Client releases and holds harmless MedPup Veterinary Concierge, LLC, its members, employees, and agents from any and all claims, damages, or liabilities arising from or related to the Trip or any medical care provided by the Clinic.

The Client agrees to indemnify MedPup against any claims brought by third parties arising from the Client's actions during the Trip.

This release does not apply to claims arising from MedPup's own gross negligence or willful misconduct.

### 9. EMERGENCY AUTHORIZATION

In the event of a medical emergency during the Trip and if the Client is not immediately available to consent, the Client authorizes the attending veterinarian at the Clinic to perform any medically necessary treatment for the Pet. MedPup will make reasonable efforts to contact the Client before any emergency treatment is performed.

### 10. PHOTO AND VIDEO CONSENT

The Client grants MedPup permission to use de-identified photographs and video of the Pet for marketing, educational, and promotional purposes. The Client's own image will not be used without separate written consent.

### 11. GOVERNING LAW

This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of the State of Florida. Any disputes shall be resolved in the courts of Broward County, Florida.

### 12. ENTIRE AGREEMENT

This Agreement constitutes the entire understanding between the parties. No modifications shall be effective unless in writing and signed by both parties.
