# MedPup Client Service Agreement

**Version 1.0 — Effective Date:** May 20, 2026
**Service Provider:** MedPup Veterinary Concierge, LLC (Florida — formation pending)
**Service Type:** Veterinary logistics coordination (NOT veterinary medical services)

---

## 1. DEFINITIONS

**"Company"** means MedPup Veterinary Concierge, LLC, a Florida limited liability company.

**"Client"** means the pet owner entering this agreement.

**"Pet"** means the animal identified in the intake form for which coordination services are requested.

**"Partner Clinic"** means an independently licensed veterinary clinic with which Company coordinates appointments.

**"Coordination Fee"** means the flat fee charged by Company, separate from any fees charged by the Partner Clinic.

**"All-In Cost Estimate"** means Company's estimated range of what Client will pay the Partner Clinic, including all disclosed and anticipated add-on charges (blood work, medications, post-operative care, etc.).

**"Price Guarantee"** means Company's commitment that if Client's final bill from the Partner Clinic exceeds the upper bound of the All-In Cost Estimate, Company will either (a) cover the excess up to the Coordination Fee amount, or (b) route Client to a backup clinic at no additional coordination fee.

---

## 2. SERVICES PROVIDED

Company agrees to provide the following coordination services:

1. **Clinic Identification:** Identify Partner Clinic(s) within driving distance (Tier 1) or via air travel (Tier 2) capable of performing the requested veterinary procedure.

2. **Price Verification:** Contact Partner Clinic to obtain current pricing, including mandatory add-on charges. Provide Client with an All-In Cost Estimate.

3. **Appointment Coordination:** Schedule the appointment with the Partner Clinic and share Client's pet medical records as authorized.

4. **Price Guarantee Administration:** If the final bill from the Partner Clinic exceeds the All-In Cost Estimate upper bound, Company will honor the Price Guarantee as defined in Section 1.

5. **Post-Procedure Follow-Up:** Provide a surgical summary from the Partner Clinic to Client's primary US veterinarian upon request.

---

## 3. SERVICES NOT PROVIDED

Company **does not** provide:

1. **Veterinary Medical Services:** Company is not a veterinary practice. All medical care — including diagnosis, treatment, surgery, and medication — is provided exclusively by independently licensed veterinarians at Partner Clinics. Company does not practice veterinary medicine, does not supervise veterinary procedures, and does not employ veterinarians.

2. **Insurance:** Company does not provide pet insurance, health insurance, or travel insurance.

3. **Financing:** Company does not provide payment plans, loans, or credit arrangements. The Coordination Fee is due at booking confirmation.

4. **Transportation:** Company does not operate vehicles or provide transportation services. Client is responsible for their own transportation to and from Partner Clinics.

5. **Boarding or Kenneling:** Company does not provide pet boarding, kenneling, or overnight care.

## 4. FEE STRUCTURE

### 4.1 Coordination Fee
Client agrees to pay Company a flat Coordination Fee as follows:

| Service Tier | Fee Range | Due |
|-------------|-----------|-----|
| Tier 1 — Domestic (spay/neuter) | $25–$50 | At booking confirmation |
| Tier 1 — Domestic (dental/surgery) | $50–$100 | At booking confirmation |
| Tier 2 — Cancun Concierge | $1,500–$2,500 | At booking confirmation |

The specific fee will be quoted in writing before Client commits to booking.

### 4.2 Partner Clinic Fees
Client understands and agrees that all fees charged by the Partner Clinic — including the All-In Cost Estimate — are paid directly by Client to the Partner Clinic. Company does not collect, process, or guarantee Partner Clinic fees beyond the Price Guarantee in Section 5.

### 4.3 Payment Methods
Coordination Fee payment accepted via: Venmo, PayPal, Zelle, or direct bank transfer. No credit card processing available at this time.

### 4.4 Refund Policy
- **Cancellation 48+ hours before appointment:** Full refund of Coordination Fee.
- **Cancellation within 48 hours:** Coordination Fee forfeited.
- **Clinic-initiated cancellation:** Full refund of Coordination Fee.
- **No-show at appointment:** Coordination Fee forfeited.

---

## 5. PRICE GUARANTEE

### 5.1 Guarantee Scope
Company guarantees that Client's total payment to the Partner Clinic will fall within the All-In Cost Estimate range provided in writing prior to booking.

### 5.2 Guarantee Remedy
If the Partner Clinic's final bill exceeds the upper bound of the All-In Cost Estimate:

1. **First remedy:** Company will identify a backup Partner Clinic capable of performing the same procedure at a price within the original All-In Cost Estimate. Company will coordinate the new appointment at no additional Coordination Fee.

2. **Second remedy (if backup unavailable):** Company will refund the Coordination Fee to Client. Company's total liability under this guarantee is capped at the Coordination Fee amount.

### 5.3 Guarantee Exclusions
The Price Guarantee does not cover:
- Additional procedures requested by Client after the All-In Cost Estimate was provided.
- Procedures performed by a non-partner veterinarian or facility.
- Emergency care, after-hours care, or specialist referral fees.
- Changes in Client's pet's medical condition requiring different or additional treatment.
- Add-on costs that were expressly disclosed as "not included" in the All-In Cost Estimate.

### 5.4 Guarantee Claim Process
Client must notify Company within 5 business days of the appointment if the final bill exceeds the All-In Cost Estimate. Company will respond within 2 business days with the applicable remedy.

## 6. CLIENT RESPONSIBILITIES

Client agrees to:

1. Provide accurate and complete information about Pet's medical history, current medications, and any known health conditions.

2. Obtain any pre-appointment diagnostics or records requested by the Partner Clinic.

3. Transport Pet to and from the Partner Clinic at Client's own expense and responsibility.

4. Pay the Partner Clinic directly for all veterinary services.

5. Read and follow all post-procedure care instructions provided by the Partner Clinic.

6. Notify Company immediately of any complications or concerns following the procedure.
