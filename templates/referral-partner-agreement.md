### 1. PURPOSE

The Practice refers clients to MedPup for coordination of affordable international veterinary care. MedPup provides concierge logistics services; the Practice remains the primary care veterinarian.

### 2. REFERRAL PROCESS

1. Practice identifies a client whose pet needs a procedure the client cannot afford at U.S. prices
2. With client consent, Practice informs client about MedPup as an option
3. Practice sends relevant medical records to MedPup (with client authorization)
4. MedPup coordinates the trip independently
5. Post-surgery, MedPup sends complete surgical report to Practice
6. Client returns to Practice for 14-day post-op follow-up

### 3. PRACTICE RESPONSIBILITIES

- Provide initial diagnosis and treatment recommendations
- Issue USDA-accredited health certificate within 10 days of travel (if applicable)
- Perform 14-day post-operative follow-up examination
- Communicate any complications to MedPup and the operating surgeon

### 4. MEDPUP RESPONSIBILITIES

- Handle all trip logistics: travel, permits, clinic scheduling, on-site concierge
- Obtain complete surgical report and forward to Practice
- Maintain open communication regarding patient status

### 5. COMPENSATION

MedPup will pay the Practice a post-operative follow-up fee of $[Amount] for the 14-day recheck examination of each referred patient who completes a trip. This fee is for actual veterinary services rendered by the Practice.

### 6. LIABILITY

The Practice has no clinical liability for procedures performed abroad. The Practice is simply informing clients of an available option. MedPup's role is logistics only. All surgical liability rests with the operating clinic and surgeon.

### 7. TERM AND TERMINATION

This Agreement continues until terminated by either party with 30 days' written notice.
