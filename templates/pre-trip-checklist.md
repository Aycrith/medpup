## 1 WEEK BEFORE

- [ ] Confirm USDA health certificate appointment with accredited vet
- [ ] Verify rabies vaccine is current (must be at least 30 days old, not expired)
- [ ] If Cancun: confirm USDA endorsement appointment
- [ ] Arrange pet sitter for other animals at home (if needed)
- [ ] Confirm ferry/flight booking with MedPup
- [ ] Submit destination import permit (MedPup handles)

## 3 DAYS BEFORE

- [ ] Pick up USDA-endorsed health certificate (original)
- [ ] Print or download: travel tickets, clinic address, concierge phone
- [ ] Confirm no medications are close to running out
- [ ] Pack pet's regular medications (enough for trip + 3 days buffer)
- [ ] Confirm medicated baths/grooming are done (if required pre-surgery)

## NIGHT BEFORE

- [ ] Remove all food after midnight. Water is okay until 6:00 AM
- [ ] Set two alarms. Plan to arrive at Port Everglades Terminal 21 by 6:00 AM (Freeport) or MIA Airport by [time] (Cancun)
- [ ] Charge phone fully. Save concierge number
- [ ] Lay out: leash, collar with ID tag, secure carrier (if small dog), poop bags

## MORNING OF TRIP

- [ ] Light walk for potty before leaving home
- [ ] Pack: health certificate (original), rabies certificate, leash, carrier, any meds, a small blanket
- [ ] Wear comfortable clothes and shoes
- [ ] Pack a light snack and water for yourself
- [ ] Bring a book or entertainment (ferry/flight waiting time)
