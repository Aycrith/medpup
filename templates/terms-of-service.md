## 4. Coordination Fee & Payment Terms

### 4.1 Our Fee

MedPup charges a flat coordination fee of **$25.00 to $100.00** per appointment. The exact fee depends on the complexity of the coordination required and will be disclosed before you commit.

### 4.2 What the Fee Covers

Your coordination fee covers:
- Clinic research and matching
- Appointment scheduling coordination
- Administrative support
- Communication facilitation with the clinic

### 4.3 What the Fee Does NOT Cover

Your coordination fee does **not** cover:
- Any veterinary medical procedures, tests, or treatments
- Medications, vaccines, or supplies
- Clinic facility fees or examination fees
- Emergency or after-hours care
- Transportation or lodging

**The clinic will bill you separately** for all veterinary services provided.

### 4.4 Payment Methods

We accept Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover, and debit cards. Payment is processed through our third-party payment processor. By providing payment information, you represent that you are authorized to use the payment method provided.

### 4.5 Refund Policy

- If MedPup is **unable to schedule** your appointment within a reasonable timeframe, you may request a full refund of your coordination fee.
- If you **cancel before an appointment is booked**, you may request a full refund of your coordination fee.
- If you **cancel after an appointment is booked** (but at least 48 hours before the appointment), you may request a 50% refund of your coordination fee.
- If you **cancel less than 48 hours before the appointment** or **fail to show up**, the coordination fee is non-refundable.
- Refunds are processed within 5–10 business days to the original payment method.

### 4.6 All-In Cost Guarantee

MedPup operates under an **All-In Cost Guarantee**. We will provide you with the total anticipated cost of your pet's procedure (including the clinic's charge and our fee) before you commit. If the clinic's actual charge exceeds the quoted amount by more than 10% (excluding unforeseen complications or additional services you authorize at the time of service), MedPup will refund the difference up to the full coordination fee.
