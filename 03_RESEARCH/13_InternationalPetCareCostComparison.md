## 13.4 COSTA RICA AS EMERGING COMPETITOR

Costa Rica's veterinary infrastructure has grown with its medical/dental tourism industry:

| Procedure | Costa Rica Price | vs. Mexico | vs. U.S. |
|-----------|-----------------|-----------|----------|
| TPLO | $1,800–$2,800 | Slightly higher | 50-65% less |
| Dental cleaning | $100–$180 | Similar | 65-80% less |
| Spay (large breed) | $120–$250 | Slightly higher | 50-80% less |

**Competitive note:** Costa Rica is an emerging pet medical tourism destination but lacks the geographic convenience of the Bahamas (3-hour ferry) or Cancun (1.5-hour flight from Miami). Its growth may accelerate as the market matures.

## 13.6 KEY INSIGHT FOR MEDPUP PRICING STRATEGY

1. **The sweet spot is TPLO/orthopedic:** This is the highest-margin, highest-demand procedure that crosses the $3,000 psychological breakpoint. It should be the hero service.
2. **Dental is the volume driver:** Lower price point, higher volume, fast procedure — ideal for filling Freeport day-trips with 4-5 pets.
3. **The medication arbitrage is an untapped market:** A "Medication Pickup" day-trip ($285 concierge fee + ferry + medication cost) could serve chronic-condition pets whose owners face $200-$500 monthly medication bills. This market has no existing competitor.
4. **Bahamas pricing is competitive with Mexico:** While slightly higher for some procedures, the absence of screwworm certificate requirements, shorter travel time, and CareCredit acceptance make it a compelling alternative to the Mexico corridor.
