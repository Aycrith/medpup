The wellness market is relevant to MedPup because it creates the preventive-to-curative pipeline:

ROUTINE WELLNESS EXAM
│
▼
ABNORMALITY DETECTED (mass, dental disease, lameness)
│
▼
DIAGNOSTIC WORKUP RECOMMENDED (bloodwork, imaging, biopsy)
│
▼
SPECIALIST REFERRAL (orthopedic surgeon, oncologist, dentist)
│
▼
SURGICAL QUOTE (3,000−3,000−12,000)
│
▼
CLIENT SEEKS ALTERNATIVE (MedPup)


Every wellness visit that detects an abnormality is a potential MedPup lead. The 66% of U.S. households owning pets and the growing emphasis on preventive care mean this funnel is expanding.

## 21.9 THE SUPPLEMENT ARBITRAGE PARALLEL

Just as surgical and medication prices differ dramatically between the U.S. and Mexico/Bahamas, supplement prices show similar patterns:

| Supplement | U.S. Retail Price | Mexico Pharmacy | Savings |
|-----------|------------------|-----------------|---------|
| Glucosamine/Chondroitin (joint) | $30–$60/month | $10–$20/month | 55–67% |
| Omega-3 fatty acids | $25–$50/month | $8–$18/month | 64–68% |
| Probiotics | $20–$40/month | $5–$15/month | 63–75% |
| CBD oil (500mg) | $40–$80/month | $15–$30/month | 60–63% |

While individual supplement savings are smaller than surgical savings, the recurring monthly nature creates substantial annual savings ($300–$800/year) — and a reason for recurring cross-border trips.

## 21.10 KEY INSIGHT FOR MEDPUP

1. **The $7.6 billion supplements market growing at 7%+ annually signals pet owners are investing in health beyond acute care.** This wellness orientation makes them more receptive to proactive cost-saving measures like MedPup.
2. **The 18% CAGR in CBD pet products is the canary in the coal mine for alternative pet health.** Pet owners are comfortable seeking non-traditional solutions — a psychographic that overlaps strongly with cross-border medical tourism acceptance.
3. **The preventive-to-curative funnel is MedPup's organic lead generation engine.** By building relationships with general practice vets (who perform wellness exams), MedPup intercepts surgical candidates at the point of diagnosis.
4. **Supplement arbitrage could be bundled into medication tourism trips.** A client refilling chronic medications could also purchase 3–6 months of supplements at 55–75% savings, further increasing trip value.
5. **The wellness market's 83% decade growth projection signals a long-term secular trend.** Pet health spending is not cyclical — it is structurally expanding as the humanization trend deepens across generations.
