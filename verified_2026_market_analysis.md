## 4. STRATEGIC CONCLUSION

### 4.1 What the Data Actually Says

1. **The market crisis is real and verified.** 52% of pet owners skipping care. 71% citing cost. $158 billion industry. NY Mag, Gallup, APPA all confirm this in 2025-2026.

2. **The cross-border concierge model is proven.** MexiVet Express has 11 employees, 7 vehicles, multiple competitors. CBX Pet Care operates the exact same "concierge + transparent pricing" model we're proposing. They exist and are growing.

3. **The California model doesn't directly translate to Florida.** Tijuana is a land border with drive-across access. Cancun requires flights. This changes the economics significantly.

4. **Cancun only works for high-ticket procedures ($4,000+ US cost).** Dental, spay/neuter, and routine care cannot justify the airfare + concierge fee. The domestic Phase 1 model wins for those.

5. **The real opportunity: Florida's "Tijuana equivalent" doesn't exist yet.** There is no east-coast cross-border pet concierge service. The closest option for Florida pet owners facing a $7,000 TPLO is... nothing. Fly to San Diego? No.

### 4.2 Recommended Strategy

**Two-tier model:**

**Tier 1: Domestic Coordination (Active Now)**
- Pinellas → South Florida clinics (ASPCA CVC, Good Care)
- $25-$100 coordination fee
- Covers the 52% who need basic affordable care
- Zero capital, zero logistics
- **Defensible:** No competitor does this with an all-in cost guarantee

**Tier 2: Cancun Surgical Concierge (Build Next)**
- Target: TPLO, spinal surgery, oncology, major tumor removal ONLY
- Fee: **$2,000 - $2,500** per client
- Target client: pet owner quoted $6,000+ for a procedure they cannot afford
- **Price anchoring:** "Your vet quoted $7,000. Our Cancun route: $2,000 concierge + $1,750 direct costs = $3,750 total. You save $3,250."
- Volume: 2-3 clients/month = $48,000-$90,000/year revenue

**What NOT to build:**
- Don't offer Cancun for dental, spay, or routine care (economics don't work)
- Don't pursue Freeport (clinic capability unverified, no competitor validation)
- Don't offer a $900 concierge fee (leaves no margin after real costs)

### 4.3 Immediate Next Actions (Validated by Data)

1. **Post the Facebook tests** — The Gallup data (73% never offered alternatives) suggests the "all-in cost guarantee" message will resonate. Test it.
2. **Lock in domestic partner clinics** — A few calls confirm capacity. The domestic model costs you nothing.
3. **Contact CBX Pet Care or MexiVet Express for a partner conversation** — Not as competition, but as a model. They've proven the concierge model works at scale.
4. **Build the Cancun tier once domestic revenue covers the first Cancun trip** — Self-fund the pilot through domestic coordination fees.
