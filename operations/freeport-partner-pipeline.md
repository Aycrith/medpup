## 5. Negotiation Framework

| MedPup's Value | Ask in Return |
|----------------|---------------|
| 20-50 pre-booked patients/year, fully vetted | 10-15% off published surgery rates |
| All communication, translation, payment issues handled | Transparent price list for common procedures |
| On-site concierge reduces clinic's staff burden | Photo consent and surgeon bio for website |
| Free marketing (clinic featured on website and social media) | Clear scheduling windows (4-hour block) |

**Fallback position:** If the clinic won't discount, ask for:
- Priority scheduling (first surgery slot of the day)
- No deposit for MedPup patients
- Written surgical report included in fee
- Direct contact with surgeon for follow-up
